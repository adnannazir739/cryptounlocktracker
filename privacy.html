Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores favorites, price alerts, unlock reminders, theme preference, onboarding status, optional local profile display settings, login session state, portfolio display data, and Google Play Premium Full Access status on your device.

The app does not ask for wallet seed phrases, private keys, exchange passwords, bank details, or crypto payments.

Data Processed By App Services

The app connects to backend and third-party services to load crypto market information, coin metadata, token unlock schedules, exchange/listing announcements, and news links. These requests may include standard technical information such as IP address, network metadata, request time, app version, and requested coin symbols. If you create an account, we process your email address, name, login provider, session token, portfolio holdings you enter, purchase entitlement status, and Community posts/comments/reactions/reports/blocks.

Payments

Premium features may be unlocked through an optional lifetime in-app purchase. Payments are processed by Google Play Billing. Crypto Unlock Tracker does not receive or store card details or Google payment credentials. We may store purchase entitlement status and a hashed purchase token so your premium access can be restored after login.

Community Content

If you use Community, your posts, comments, display name, likes/dislikes, reports, and blocks may be stored on our backend. Users can report objectionable posts/comments and block authors. Reported content may be reviewed, hidden, or removed to protect users and comply with platform policies.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app can show price alerts and unlock reminders that you configure. Notification settings are controlled by your device and app settings.

Sharing

Crypto Unlock Tracker does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers used to operate the app, such as hosting providers, Google Play Billing, market data providers, listing/news sources, and analytics or logging services if enabled in the future.

Retention And Deletion

On-device data remains until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Backend logs are retained only as long as needed for security, debugging, abuse prevention, and service reliability. If you create an account, you can request deletion in the app from the top-right menu by choosing Delete account, or contact contact@vortixvpn.com. Account deletion removes your account, sessions, stored portfolio holdings, and Community content from our active backend database, except records we must retain for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or payment-dispute purposes.

Security

Network traffic uses HTTPS where supported. API keys and provider credentials should be kept on the backend and not embedded in the Android app. No internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure.

Children

Crypto Unlock Tracker is not directed to children and should be targeted in Play Console as a general crypto/finance information app for users old enough to understand financial market risk in their region.

Financial Disclaimer

Market prices, unlock schedules, supply figures, listing announcements, and news links may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate. The app is provided for information only and should not be used as the sole basis for financial decisions.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above and, where required, notifying users in the app or on this website.
