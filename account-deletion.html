Account Deletion

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This page explains how Crypto Unlock Tracker users can delete their account and request deletion of associated data.

Delete Your Account In The App

  1. Open Crypto Unlock Tracker on your Android device.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Open the top-right menu.
  4. Select Delete account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

After confirmation, the app sends the account deletion request to our backend service.

Request Deletion By Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to contact@vortixvpn.com from the email address linked to your Crypto Unlock Tracker account.

Use this subject line:

Delete Crypto Unlock Tracker Account

Include the email address used for your account so we can verify and process the request.

Data Deleted

Account deletion may delete or anonymize the following data linked to your account:

  • Account profile details such as name and email login record
  • Active login sessions and authentication tokens
  • Portfolio holdings entered by you
  • Favorites, alerts, reminders, and saved app preferences linked to your account
  • Community posts, comments, reactions, reports, and blocks where deletion is technically and legally allowed
  • Premium entitlement records stored by our backend, except records needed to verify Google Play purchases

Data That May Be Kept

Some records may be retained for a limited period where needed for legal, security, fraud prevention, abuse investigation, payment dispute, tax, accounting, or Google Play purchase verification purposes. Google Play purchase records are handled by Google under Google Play terms and policies.

Processing Time

In-app deletion requests are normally processed automatically. Email deletion requests are reviewed and processed as soon as practical. We may ask for reasonable verification before deleting an account to protect users from unauthorized deletion requests.

Contact

For account deletion, privacy, or support questions, contact contact@vortixvpn.com.
